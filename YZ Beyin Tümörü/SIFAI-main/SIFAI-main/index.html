About Use

Şifai is an innovative platform that combines technology and medicine for brain tumor detection. Developed as part of the 2024-2025 research project, this platform allows users to upload MRI images and detect potential tumors using AI-powered algorithms.

Our goal is to accelerate medical imaging processes, increase accuracy, and improve the quality of life by making healthcare more accessible. Şifai aims to provide pioneering solutions in health technology by supporting early diagnosis.
